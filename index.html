How to use IGTracker

Step-by-step guide to export your Instagram data and upload it here.

1
Request your data from Instagram
Open Instagram, go to Settings & Privacy → Account Centre → Your information & permissions → Download your information. Select JSON format and time range "All time".
Settings → Download Your Information → JSON format → Create files
2
Wait for the download link
Instagram will send an email with a download link. Depending on your account size, this can take anywhere from a few minutes to 48 hours.
3
Extract and locate your files
Unzip the downloaded archive. Navigate into the extracted folder and find the relevant JSON files.
connections/followers_and_following/followers_1.jsonRequired
connections/followers_and_following/following.jsonRequired
connections/followers_and_following/recently_unfollowed_profiles.jsonOptional
your_instagram_activity/likes/liked_posts.jsonOptional
your_instagram_activity/comments/post_comments.jsonOptional
your_instagram_activity/searches/word_or_phrase_searches.jsonOptional
4
Upload and analyze
Click each upload card at the top of this page to select the corresponding file. Once the two required files are uploaded, click Analyze Data to see your full report.
5
Your data never leaves your device
Everything is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server. You can even disconnect from the internet before uploading and the tool will still work perfectly.
6
Export your results
Each tab has an Export CSV button. Use this to save any list to a spreadsheet for further analysis, or to keep a timestamped record of your account's relationship graph over time.
